About Us

Who we are.

We're a tight-knit team of 8 student engineers, programmers, and designers competing in the FIRST Tech Challenge. From CAD to code, from drive practice to outreach — every part of our robot and our team is built by us.

Our mission is to make competitive robotics accessible, fun, and rigorous. We believe great engineering happens when curiosity meets craftsmanship, and we work hard to embody both on and off the field.
